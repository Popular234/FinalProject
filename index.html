The Ingredients:
2-3 medium overripe banans
1/3 cup brown butter
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 pinch of salt
3/4 cup of sugar (add more or less to taste)
1 large egg, well beat
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
Steps to Baking
PreHeat your oven and butter your pan:
Preheat the oven to 350°F and butter a pan of 8 x 4 inchs
Mash the banans and add your butter:
In a mixing bowl mash your banans with a fork until one consistency and stir in your browned butter into the banana mash
Mix in the dry and remainig mixture:
Mix in the baking soda and salt mixture. Stir in your sugar, egg, and vanilla extract, and finally your flour.
Bake Bread:
pour your batter into your loaf pan. Bake this for 55 to 65 minutes at 350°F, or until a toothbpick or wooden skewer inserted into the center comes out clean. a few dry crumbs will be okay; if the batter is wet this is not. if the outside is browned before the inside is cooked loosly cover with tinfoil and continue baking.
Cool Before Serve:
Remove from oven and let remain in pan for fe minutes, then carefully remove from pan and allow to cool to room temp before serving. then finally slice and serve
if the bread is wrapped well it can be kept in room temp for 4 days but for longer storage keep in the refirgerater for up to 5 days or freeze the loaf.
